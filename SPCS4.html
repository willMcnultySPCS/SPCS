The dead drop:

To make sure the Elves are not hacking the website, you must complete the dead drop. One of our pony agents is hiding close by and has the answer to the final clue. You must deliver a carrot (her favourite Christmas snack) to the back garden. Leave it there and go back inside. You must not watch because she is very shy and must keep her identity secret. Once you get the all clear, you can go and retrieve the clue she has left you.
